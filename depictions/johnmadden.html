Welcome to John Madden football.
(for iOS 6 devices)

John Madden! is a theme that replaces every system icon (and more, such as extra icons and wallpapers) with a picture of John Madden from the videogame John Madden Football '92.

John Madden.theme - basically the full package (excluding the wallpapers).

John Madden Wallpapers.theme - John Madden wallpapers. Use this along with the full theme option for the best experience.

John Madden (lite edition).theme - a lite version of the John Madden theme, which only replaces the Cydia icon. Use this if you want the John Madden experience without over-doing it.

You can notify me of any apps you want themed (send bundle IDs) by emailing me at hp-repo@outlook.com
