John Madden!

  • Welcome to John Madden football.
    (for iOS 6 devices)
  • This theme replaces every system icon (and more, such as extra icons and wallpapers) with a picture of John Madden from the videogame John Madden Football '92.

Winterboard Info

  • John Madden
    This theme option is basically the whole package, excluding the wallpapers.
  • John Madden Wallpapers
    This theme option adds John Madden wallpapers (for both the lockscreen and homescreen) so you can look at a stretched version of John Madden at any moment. You can enable this along with the full theme option for the best effect.
  • John Madden (lite edition)
    This theme option themes only one icon (Cydia), in case the full theme option is too much for you. With this option, you can have John Madden on your homescreen without having to look through other John Maddens.
